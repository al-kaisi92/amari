Important policies and guidelines for your visit to Amari Head Spa

Booking & Cancellation

  • 24-hour cancellation notice is required for all appointments. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a 50% cancellation fee.
  • No-shows (clients who do not arrive for their appointment without prior notice) will be charged the full treatment price.
  • Rescheduling is free of charge when done with at least 24 hours notice. We're happy to find a new time that works for you.
  • Booking confirmation will be sent via email or SMS. Please ensure your contact details are correct when booking.
  • We reserve the right to cancel or reschedule appointments due to unforeseen circumstances. We will contact you as soon as possible to arrange an alternative time.

Arrival & Timing

  • Please arrive 5-10 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. This allows time for consultation and preparation.
  • Late arrivals may result in shortened treatment time to avoid delays for subsequent clients. Full payment will still be required.
  • If you are running late, please call us as soon as possible at 07442 397456.
  • Arrivals more than 15 minutes late may be treated as a no-show, and we reserve the right to cancel the appointment with a full charge applied.
  • For your comfort, we recommend avoiding heavy meals immediately before your treatment.

  • Vouchers are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase unless otherwise stated.
  • Prices are subject to change without prior notice. The price displayed at the time of booking will be honoured.

Health & Safety

  • Inform us of allergies or health conditions before your treatment. This helps us customise your experience.
  • Patch tests may be required for certain treatments, particularly for clients with sensitive skin or known allergies.
  • We reserve the right to refuse or modify a service if we believe it may be harmful to your health or safety.
  • Treatments are not suitable for clients with active scalp infections, open wounds, severe sunburn, or certain medical conditions.
  • If you are pregnant or nursing, please inform us when booking so we can advise on suitable treatments.
  • We maintain the highest standards of hygiene and cleanliness. All equipment is sanitised between clients.
