Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect during my first visit?
Your first visit begins with a personalized consultation and scalp analysis. Our expert therapists will assess your hair and scalp condition, discuss your concerns, and recommend the perfect treatment for your needs. You'll then enjoy a luxurious, relaxing experience in our tranquil spa environment.
How often should I get a head spa treatment?
For optimal results, we recommend treatments every 2-4 weeks. However, the frequency depends on your individual scalp health, hair goals, and lifestyle. Some clients prefer weekly sessions for intensive care, while others visit monthly for maintenance.
Can I get a head spa treatment if I have sensitive skin or allergies?
Absolutely! We offer vegan options and can customize treatments for sensitive skin. Please inform us of any allergies or skin sensitivities during your consultation, and we'll select products specifically suited to your needs.
Will my hair be professionally styled after the treatment?
All treatments include a rough dry (towel and gentle blow dry to remove excess moisture), but not a professional blow dry or styling. If you'd like professional styling, please let us know when booking and we can arrange this as an additional service.
What's the difference between the Exotic Bliss treatments?
Exotic Bliss (60min) is our signature treatment perfect for first-timers. Exotic Bliss Signature (90min) offers extended massage time and more intensive conditioning. Ultimate Exotic Bliss (1.5hrs) adds facial massage and acupressure for the most comprehensive experience.
Can men enjoy head spa treatments?
Yes! Head spa treatments are beneficial for everyone, regardless of gender or hair length. Many of our male clients love the stress-relief and scalp health benefits. All treatments can be customized to suit individual needs and preferences.

📋 Important Booking Information

Before booking your treatment, please familiarise yourself with our booking policies. We require 24-hour notice for cancellations and ask that you arrive 5-10 minutes early to ensure you receive your full treatment time.
