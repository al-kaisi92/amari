Welcome to the AMARI Head Spa Ritual

Discover a sanctuary of serenity and luxury at Amari Head Spa, where ancient traditions and modern indulgence blend seamlessly. At AMARI Head Spa Ritual, we have crafted a truly unique experience that combines the best of Thai head massage techniques with elements of Japanese head spa rituals, creating a one-of-a-kind journey for your senses.

Our Unique Approach

The AMARI Head Spa Ritual truly special is the meticulous fusion of Thai and Japanese techniques. Our foundation lies in the healing power of Thai head massage, focusing on lymphatic stimulation across the head, neck, and shoulders to release tension and promote circulation.

We then elevate this experience with the delicate artistry of Japanese head spa elements: the soothing halo water flow, gentle feather touches, Sound Bowl and calming aromatherapy scents like coconut oil, all designed to transport you to a state of bliss.

Wellness & Sustainability

We believe in nurturing both our clients and the planet. That's why we offer vegan friendly product options, ensuring that our treatments are as kind to the environment as they are to your senses. Every aspect of the AMARI Head Spa Ritual experience is thoughtfully designed to be both luxurious and responsible.

Comprehensive Head Spa Benefits

Experience transformative benefits for your scalp, hair, and overall well-being

Head spas offer a multitude of benefits for both physical and mental well-being, including promoting scalp health, encouraging hair growth, and relieving stress and tension. These treatments can also enhance hair texture and shine, boost mental clarity, and improve sleep quality.

Scalp & Hair Health

  • Scalp cleansing and detoxification: Deep cleanse the scalp to remove dirt, product build-up, and dead skin cells, promoting a healthier environment for hair follicles
  • Improved blood circulation: Massage techniques stimulate blood flow to the scalp, nourishing hair follicles and potentially encouraging hair growth
  • Hair nourishment and repair: Deep conditioning treatments and hydrating masks help repair damaged hair, restore moisture, and enhance shine and softness
  • Balancing scalp oil production: Techniques to regulate sebum production, helping to prevent greasy hair and scalp discomfort

Stress & Tension Relief

  • Deep relaxation: Massage techniques focus on acupressure points on the scalp, which are rich in nerve endings, helping to release tension and reduce stress
  • Headache and migraine relief: Targeted massage can help reduce muscle tension in the scalp, neck, and shoulders, potentially alleviating headache pain
  • Improved sleep quality: The relaxation provided by a head spa can contribute to deeper sleep and a more refreshed feeling
  • Enhanced mental clarity and focus: The soothing environment and therapeutic touch can help clear the mind, reduce anxiety, and improve focus

Enhanced Beauty & Wellness

  • Enhanced hair texture and shine: Deep conditioning treatments make hair softer, shinier, and more manageable
  • Aromatherapy: Incorporate aromatherapy to enhance the sensory experience, potentially calming the mind and uplifting the spirits
  • Mood enhancement: The combination of massage and aromatherapy can lead to a more positive and relaxed mood
